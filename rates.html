Booking FAQ

Common questions about rates, deposits, and what to expect on the river.

Do I need a fishing license?

Yes — both Oregon and Washington require a valid fishing license. We recommend purchasing online before your trip. We can point you to the right license for the species and location.

What should I bring?

Dress in layers for changing Gorge weather, bring rain gear, sunglasses, snacks, drinks, and a cooler for your fillets. We provide all fishing equipment and safety gear.

Is a deposit required?

A deposit may be required to hold peak-season dates. We'll confirm availability and payment details when you call or submit a booking request.

What if the weather is bad?

Our heated boats keep you comfortable in most conditions. If unsafe weather arises, we'll work with you to reschedule at no extra charge.
