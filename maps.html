    Travel Tips

    • Arrive 15 minutes before your scheduled launch time
    • Parking is available at most public boat ramps
    • From Portland: ~1 hour to Hood River via I-84
    • From Seattle: ~3.5 hours via I-90 and US-97

    What to Expect

    Your guide will meet you at the boat ramp with the boat in the water and ready to go. Look for our heated guide boat with Mid Columbia River Guide Service markings. Call (509) 490-4666 if you need help finding the launch.
